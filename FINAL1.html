About Us

Driven by Change: Meet the Teenage Reformers Behind CarboNeutralizer

WHO ARE WE?

We are Meenal Gupta & Eshvi Singh, from Team CO2nsistent Reformers — two teenagers driven by purpose and passion to fight one of the biggest challenges of our time: carbon emissions.

Our awareness began with what we saw around us — blackened hills scarred by forest fires, rising temperatures, and a planet in distress. Curious and concerned, we dove deep into research and uncovered shocking facts about how everyday choices contribute to a larger climate crisis.

What we found didn’t just open our eyes — it changed our mindset.

That’s why we’re here: to simplify complex climate facts, spread awareness about carbon footprints, and inspire action through meaningful content. From sharing daily facts to building this website, we’re committed to making carbon consciousness a part of everyday life.

We may be young, but our goal is clear —
A cleaner, greener, carbon-conscious world.

Our Campaign - Decade of Deeds

Take a look at some of our past and ongoing environmental campaign days.

Day 1 - Binge & Breathe 📺🌍

We revealed how binge-watching also leads to CO₂ emissions — from energy-hungry screens to streaming servers. We asked people questions like, “What’s your binge record — 5 episodes, 10, or a whole season?” and sparked fun, eye-opening conversations.

Day 2 – Hidden Emissions 🎮📦📺

We exposed how everyday habits like gaming, streaming, and opting for fast shipping quietly add to CO₂ emissions — without most people even realizing it. From high-power consoles to speedy deliveries, we showed how convenience often comes with a hidden carbon cost.

Day 3 – Travel Smarter, Breathe Better 🚗🌱

We focused on how transport is a major contributor to CO₂ emissions — but also how easily we can cut it down. By carpooling, using public transport, or simply walking more, we can make a big difference. We shared simple facts and asked relatable questions like how people commute daily, sparking practical, solution-based conversations.

Day 4 - Screensavers & CO₂

We educated people online about how even idle screensavers consume power and add to CO₂ emissions. 💡 Simple fix? Turn off your screen or disable screensavers when not in use. Small changes = big impact for the planet. 🌍💻

Day 5 – The Carbon Cost of Emails 📬

We revealed how even a single email leaves a carbon footprint — thanks to the energy used by servers and data storage. From unread newsletters to overflowing inboxes, it all adds up. We encouraged people to delete unnecessary mails, unsubscribe from spam, and keep their inboxes clean to cut down on digital CO₂ emissions.

Day 6 – The Hidden Cost of Fast Fashion 👕🌏

We uncovered how fast fashion takes a toll on the planet. From CO₂-heavy production to wasteful trends, the fashion industry is a major polluter. We urged people to choose sustainable clothing, repeat outfits proudly, and think twice before buying into fast-changing fads.

Day 7 - Hidden Habits, Heavy Impact

We showed how small habits—like using plastic, overcharging devices, leaving lights/fans on, and relying on chemical products—quietly increase our carbon footprint. Simple switches can lead to a greener tomorrow!

Day 8 - The Cost of Convenience

We highlighted how extra packaging, fossil-fueled transport, fast shipping, and separate deliveries for the same order all add up—fueling our carbon footprint in the name of convenience.

Day 9 - Wasting Food=Wasting the Planet

We revealed how food waste isn’t just about wasted meals—it leads to wasted water, energy, transport, and releases harmful greenhouse gases as it rots, adding massively to our carbon footprint.

Day 10 - Print Less,Save More

We talked about how overprinting wastes paper, energy, and ink—draining natural resources and increasing carbon emissions. A simple "think before you print" can go a long way!
